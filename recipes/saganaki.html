Saganaki

Description

Flame this pain-fried kasseri cheese with brandy, cracked-pepper, and fresh-squeezed lemon.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of kasseri cheese
  • milk
  • lemon
  • flour
  • butter
  • brandy
  • cracked pepper

Directions

  1. Slice saganaki into 4x4 inch squares. Dip saganaki into bowl of milk and then into bowl of flour. Repeat milk/four dipping once again. Shake off excess flour
  2. cook squares into buttered frying pan on low to medium heat. flip occasionally to cook throughout without burning outside.
  3. flame table-side with brandy, cracked-pepper, and fresh squeezed lemon.
