Saganaki

Description

Flame this pain-fried kasseri cheese with brandy, cracked-pepper, and fresh-squeezed lemon.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of kasseri cheese
  • milk
  • lemon
  • flour
  • butter
  • brandy
  • cracked pepper

Directions

  1. Slice kasseri into 4x4 inch squares (1/2 inch thick). Dip kasserri square into bowl of milk and then into bowl of flour. Repeat milk/four dipping once again. Shake off excess flour. Repeat for all squares
  2. Cook battered kasseri squares into buttered frying pan on low to medium heat. Flip occasionally to cook throughout without burning outside.
  3. Flame table-side in skillet with butter, brandy, cracked-pepper, and fresh squeezed lemon.
